About

ICTROPS UNMUL 2025 brings together researchers, practitioners, and industry partners to present advances in information and engineering technologies with practical impacts for Borneo and beyond. The program features peer reviewed papers, invited talks, and panels focused on applied innovation.

At a glance

  • When: 03 December 2025
  • Where: Samarinda, Indonesia
  • Mode: Hybrid
  • Language: English

Topics

  • Computer Vision and Biomedical Computing
  • Natural Language Processing
  • IoT and Sensor Networks
  • Cloud, Edge, and Distributed Systems
  • Cybersecurity and Blockchain
  • Software Engineering and DevOps
  • Smart City and Smart Village
  • Geospatial and Remote Sensing

Submission Guidelines

  • Papers must be original and not under review elsewhere.
  • Length 6–10 pages, including references, single column format.
  • Use the official template. Ensure anonymity if double blind review.
  • Submit as PDF via the Submission Page.
  • At least one author must register and present.
